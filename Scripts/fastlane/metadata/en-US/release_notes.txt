About this release:

Note: This is the last version of the app that will support iOS 12. Starting version 16.7, your app will require iOS 13 or higher.

See stats on your Home Screen: Display your WordPress site stats for the day on your iOS 14 device’s Home Screen with new Today Widgets.

Track your site activity: Filter and view items in your Activity Log — like backups, plugin updates, and published posts and pages — by date range and activity type.

Get to know your app: Learn the features in your app with an improved Quick Start tour, which guides you through steps like reviewing site pages and editing your homepage.
